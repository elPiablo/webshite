    Gateway from the soul

    They say that if there's one thing that's sure in this universe it's change. If there's one thing that hasn't changed about me, it's got to be my love for music.

    Fugazi and AC/DC just keep coming back as favourites! That goes for listening as well as for inspiration on the bass or just music making in general. Victor Wooten. Well, he's the guy that was born with a bass in his hands. And he knows music, just like Cedric!

    The wider world. Home and Away

    I grew up in the country, so I was at least always aware of nature, and special thanks to my mam, she made a point of imlicitly teaching about it. I'm not sure if I can attribute that to getting into the environmental movement.

    Dr. Vandana Shiva and Eva Dassen are two influencers that spring to mind. Greta of course as well!

    Who's ye Mama?

    XR Extinction Rebellion is something I'm proud to be a part of. It's not an easy ride though.
